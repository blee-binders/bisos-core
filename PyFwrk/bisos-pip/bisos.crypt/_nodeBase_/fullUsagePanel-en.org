** +
** Scope of bisos.crypt is  bpoVault.py  bpoGpg.py bpoSym.py
** Each BPO has a ~vault repo~  (bpo/vault) and a ~secrets~ (bpo/secrets) repo.
** Within bpo/vaults are series of keepass databases. The bpo/vaults/bisos is one such such database.
** bpo/vaults/bisos  is created when not there. bpo/vaults/bisos  password is kept outside of this platform.
** bpo/vaults/bisos  password is encrypted with bpo/gpgKeys/bisos/vault key.
** Encrypted password for bpo/vaults/bisos is kept in bpo/ramz/vaults-bisos.gpg
** --- Encrypted File Parameters ---
** fpsGpGId is stored in fpsVault.
** fpsSymCipherKey is created based on fpsSymClearKey (perhaps based on a secret convention)
** fpsSymCipherKey is stored in fpsVault.
** fpsSymCipherKey is used to symCrypt fps
** =============
** bpf.fp module uses bisos.bpoVault to obtain fpsSymCipherKey and uses bisos.bpoGpg to symCrypt the  fp.
