** What this panel is
Install guidance for the *NVIDIA GeForce RTX 5060 Ti* (Blackwell, =GB206=, PCI =10de:2d04=)
on *Debian 13 (Trixie)*, UEFI + Secure Boot ON. Blackwell needs a *570+* driver AND the
*open* kernel modules; stock Trixie =nvidia-driver 550.xx= does not drive this card.
