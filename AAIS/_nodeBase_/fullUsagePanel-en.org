**
** AAIS is layered in 3 parts.
** =AAIS-Platform= :: /bisos/platform
** =AAIS-BPOs= :: Activated AAIS-BPOs
** =aaSis= :: Service instaces of an AAIS-BPO.
**
** Concept: *AAIS Materialization*
Given the AAIS-BPO, all SIs are materialized
** Concept: **AAIS-BPO's live Repo*
Each AAIS-BPO has a "live" repo.
The ~bpo/live/platform dir contains -- containerBpoId, ip-addr
The ~bpo/live/si/plone3 dir contains -- user, passwd
** Concept: *Target -- As Live Or Here*
Each AAIS-BPO has a var/target base.
var/target/targetMode is a FP. It is either "live" or "here"
var/target/cur -- Mirrors ~bpo/live structure reflecting targetMode
