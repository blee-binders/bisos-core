*** 
*** In Place: Basic BISOS
*** In Place: bystar account (being used)
*** 
*** Create and Launch privGitServer (a gitlab instance) on your intranet
***    Perhaps using siteGitServerManage.sh
*** 
*** Configure the privGitServer info including secretKey using 
***       siteDefaultsManage.sh -h -v -n showRun -i default_gitServer
*** 
*** Use siteBxoDefaultSetup.sh to:
***    - Create prs_bisos  --- As a default pseudo real system to be parrent of the rest
***    - Create pir_privRegistrar  --- So that private bxe-s can be registered
***    - Create pis_defaultSite  --- A default site for multi-system usage
