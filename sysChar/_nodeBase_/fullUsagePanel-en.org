####+END
** +
**  The purpose of SysChar BPO is to specify the seed for a "renewable" container.
** For example, the same BxCO can be rematerialized based on
**   - a new rev of a distro or a distro (debian vs ubuntu)
**   - a different configuration of cpus and RAM and disk (sizing)
**   - a new type of vitualization technology (libvirt vs virtualbox)
** -------
** Realization: Creation of BPOs
** Activation:  BPO Activation of SysChar
** Reification:  Either of Realization or Activation, resulting into a BPO
** Materialization:  Creation of a Box or a Guest VM from a BPO -- resulting in a runable Platform
** Provisoning:  The process of combining Realization/Activation and Materialization
** Platform: Debian + BISOS + other stuff
** Capabilities: Faculties (Features) that can be added to a Platform
** Composition: Addition of Capabilities to a Platform such that it can be Re-Materialized
** Deployment: The process of powering on (starting/booting) a Box or a VM
** Stationing: The process of auto-Deployment of Boxes and VMs
** Sealing: The process of minimizing access to a deployed platform and facilitation of intrusion  detection.
** -B|
