** +
** The scope of Generic Guests is:
1) Creation and management of entries in /bisos/admin/sysChar/generics/
2) Realization of generic guest bpos with sysCharGenericRealize.sh
3) Activation of generic guest bpos in a container
4) Materialization of  generic guest bpos
** Usually needed for a new release of Debian. Prior to this vagrant boxes are built with:
/bisos/git/auth/bxRepos/blee-binders/bisos-core/virtualization/vagrant/lcaVagrantBoxBuild.sh/fullUsagePanel-en.org
and tried out using
/bisos/git/auth/bxRepos/blee-binders/bisos-core/virtualization/vagrant/lcaVagrantBoxRun.sh/fullUsagePanel-en.org
